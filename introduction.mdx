## PureAI Platform

PureAI offers a complete set of tools for developers who want to create advanced applications with artificial intelligence. Our platform consists of two completely distinct and independent products:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="PureRouter"
    icon="router"
    href="/purerouter/overview"
  >
    Independent product for intelligent LLM routing, optimizing cost, performance, and response quality.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="PureCPP"
    icon="code"
    href="/purecpp/loaders/introduction"
  >
    Separate product that offers a high-performance RAG framework in C++ for document processing and information retrieval.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Main Product Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="PureRouter: Intelligent LLM routing">
    PureRouter is an independent product that allows directing queries to the most suitable models based on criteria such as cost, performance, and quality. This optimizes resource usage and improves user experience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="PureCPP: High-performance RAG framework">
    PureCPP is a separate product that offers a complete set of tools for document processing, chunking, embedding, and information retrieval, all with the efficiency of C++.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Product Characteristics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="PureRouter: Cost and quality optimization">
    PureRouter was specifically designed to manage and optimize the use of different language models, allowing you to balance costs and quality according to your specific needs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="PureCPP: Multiple format processing">
    PureCPP offers support for various document formats, including PDF, DOCX, TXT, and web content, facilitating integration with different data sources.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Products with independent scalability">
    Each product has its own architecture designed to scale according to your project's needs, maintaining high performance even with large data volumes. You can use one or both products, depending on your needs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Get Started Now

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Using PureRouter">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Install PureRouter">
        Follow the [installation guide](/purerouter/installation) to set up PureRouter in your environment.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure your first routing">
        Learn to create [routing profiles](/purerouter/guides/routing-profiles) to direct queries to the most suitable models.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Deploy your application">
        Follow our [deployment guide](/purerouter/guides/deployments) to put your application into production.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Using PureCPP">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Set up the environment for PureCPP">
        Follow the [setup guide](/setup) to prepare your C++ development environment.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Get started with PureCPP">
        Learn the basic concepts in the [quickstart guide](/purecpp/quickstart).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Explore document loaders">
        Learn about the different options to [load documents](/purecpp/loaders/introduction) in your RAG project.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Using both products">
    <Note>
      PureRouter and PureCPP are completely independent products that can be used separately or together, depending on your needs.
    </Note>
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Decide which product meets your needs">
        Evaluate whether you need LLM routing (PureRouter), RAG capabilities (PureCPP), or both.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install the products separately">
        Follow the specific installation guides for each product you decided to use.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Integrate as needed">
        Although they are distinct products, you can integrate them into your workflow as needed.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
